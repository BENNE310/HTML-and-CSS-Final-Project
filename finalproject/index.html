DISCLAIMER: Astrology is NOT a science. The universe is infinitely vast and thus infinitely complex. Any human being who claims to have some higher understanding of human fate or the meaning of our existence is almost certainly being disingenuous. People, as well, are wildly complicated, and who we are as people is determined by far more than the month we were born, to think it would be that simple would be an insult to the very idea of human nature. That being said, Astrology is a tradition and belief that many share, and its history goes back to before the dawn of many modern religions. It is important to be respectful of people's beliefs even if you do not share them. And some people may simply find speculation on the meaning of the stars somewhat entertaining. Always remember to take everything with a grain of salt, and respect others, even if you don't come from the same base of understanding.
